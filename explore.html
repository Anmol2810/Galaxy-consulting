Our Cleaning Services

At Galaxy Consulting INC., we offer a wide range of professional cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of both residential and commercial clients. Whether you need routine cleaning, deep cleaning, or specialized services, our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results that exceed your expectations.

Our Services Include:

  • Residential Cleaning: Regular or deep cleaning services for a spotless home, covering kitchen, bathroom, windows, and more.
  • Commercial Cleaning: Customized cleaning solutions for offices, providing a clean and productive work environment.
  • Deep Cleaning: A thorough, top-to-bottom clean, perfect for seasonal cleaning or special occasions.
  • Post-Construction Cleaning: Removing debris and dust after renovations, leaving the space spotless and ready for use.
  • Carpet and Upholstery Cleaning: Expert cleaning of carpets and furniture to remove dirt, stains, and allergens.
  • Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning: Cleaning services to ensure a fresh start for new occupants or to leave a clean space behind.
  • Eco-Friendly Cleaning: We use environmentally safe products that protect both your health and the planet.
